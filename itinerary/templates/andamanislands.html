Day 1 in Andaman Islands:

8:00 AM: Begin your day with a scrumptious breakfast at Bharat Bhagya Restaurant. Savor local dishes like idli, dosa, or pancakes with chutney.

10:00 AM: Visit the historic Cellular Jail, a symbol of India's freedom struggle. Witness the sound and light show in the evening.

1:00 PM: Indulge in a delightful lunch at The Brown Tree Snake Restaurant. Try authentic Andamanese dishes such as fish curry, crab xacuti, and rice.

2:30 PM: Explore Chatham Saw Mill, the largest sawmill in Asia, and discover the mangrove forests while observing the wood processing.

4:00 PM: Relax at Kala Pathar Beach and enjoy the stunning sunset views.

7:00 PM: End your day with an exquisite dinner at The Dining Room at The Park, offering international cuisine and fresh seafood.
