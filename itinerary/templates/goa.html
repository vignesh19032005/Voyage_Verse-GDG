Day 1 in Goa:

8:00 AM: Begin your day with a hearty breakfast at Thothamani Restaurant (near Panjim), famous for its Goan specialties like pork vindaloo and serradura.

10:00 AM: Visit Fort Aguada, a Portuguese fortress located in Sinquerim, known for its stunning views of the Arabian Sea and lighthouse.

1:00 PM: Savor a traditional Goan lunch at Ritz Classic (near Dona Paula), offering an array of seafood dishes, rice bowls, and regional delicacies.

2:30 PM: Explore Old Goa's historical sites, including Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral, and Church of St. Francis of Assisi.

7:00 PM: Experience a delightful dinner at Gunpowder (Fontainhas), a popular restaurant featuring authentic South Indian dishes with a modern twist. Try their dosas, idlis, or fish fry.
